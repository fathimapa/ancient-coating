Polyurethane Coating

Polyurethane coatings provide a high-gloss, UV-resistant finish ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications. They are known for flexibility and weather protection.

Benefits:

  • Excellent UV and weather resistance
  • Flexible and impact-resistant
  • Long-lasting shine
  • Available in various colors

Applications:

  • Metal structures
  • Vehicles and machinery
  • Outdoor furniture
